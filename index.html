About Me

Abigail Lockwood

To me, illustration is about bringing someone else's ideas into the world. I believe that everyone has a vision in their mind, a story they want to tell, and it's my job to make it reality.

I've studied digital drawing for over four years, focusing on illustration for the past two. My specialty is children's book illustration, although I have dipped my toes into several areas of 2D art and design throughout college. School projects that I have worked on include video game concept art, platform game assets, storyboards, animatics, and motion graphics. The simplistic beauty of still image illustration, however, holds the most appeal for me.

My past work includes over 35 page illustrations for a children's book entitled "The Value of Rugby," which was completed and published in 2018. I enjoyed working on the project so much that I decided I wanted to hone my skills as an illustrator and help other authors bring their vision to life. This was all while still attending college and holding a part time job as a waitress, so the illustrator career would have to be put on hold until I at least obtained a degree. Nearly 2 years later, my digital drawing skills have improved and I am projected to graduate in June of 2020 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Digital Communication Arts from Oregon State University.

If you have an idea in your head that you need some help brining into reality, send me an email, or you can contact me on instagram.
